why mirror?

mirror solves a problem - sharing channel content with teams on other slack instances without having to invite slack guests or creating and maintaining extra permanent accounts.

mirror bot commands:

@mirror mirror <remote-team-url>
@mirror status
@mirror help
@mirror unmirror <remote-team-url>

recent_actors
why a bot and not an app?

Slack bots have the right security profile - they're only available once invited, only have access to the channels they're in, and speak only when spoken to.

call_split
share the content - not the channel

It's not a shared channel, you mirror an existing slack channel with an existing slack channel from a external team. 

device_hub
how it works

once installed, invited and linked, the mirror bot forwards all the messages, files and attachments to the remote slack channel, and vice-versa.
